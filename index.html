RFC TEC is Brisbane's go-to performance automotive fabrication workshop in Archerfield QLD, specialising in precision TIG welding and 100% custom metalwork for serious builds across South East Queensland.

Every project that leaves this workshop starts as raw material and an idea. No catalog parts, no bolt-on kits, no shortcuts. We design, cut, form, fit and TIG weld everything in-house using precision equipment. From engine and transmission swaps to full custom turbo kits, roll cages and chassis modifications for Brisbane, Logan and Gold Coast cars — if you can imagine it, we can build it.

[ FAQ ]

QUESTIONS WE HEAR A LOT

Real answers for performance builders in Brisbane and SEQ. Still have questions? Call or text us.

How do I get a quote for a custom turbo kit, roll cage or engine mounts?

Call or text 0412 345 678 or use the form on this page. Tell us your vehicle, what you're building and any photos or CAD you have. We usually reply with a quote within 24–48 hours. The more detail you give upfront, the faster and more accurate the quote.

What vehicles and projects do you work on?

Anything performance related — street, drift, drag, track or show cars. We regularly do engine/transmission swaps, full custom turbo setups, widebody chassis prep, roll cages and one-off parts that mainstream workshops won't touch. Brisbane, Logan, Gold Coast and Sunshine Coast builders are all welcome.

Do you install the parts you fabricate?

Yes. We can do the full install in-house in Archerfield or supply the finished parts for you or your workshop to fit. Many customers bring their car for the complete job — engine mounts, intercooler, exhaust and cage in one visit.

Are your roll cages certified?

We build to FIA and SFI specifications. We can supply the engineering paperwork required for most sanctioning bodies and club events in Queensland and Australia.

How long does a typical project take?

Simple mounts or piping: 1–2 weeks. Full custom turbo kits or mini tubs: 3–6 weeks. Complete roll cage + chassis work: 4–10 weeks depending on complexity. We give realistic timelines upfront and send progress photos.

Do you travel or only work in Archerfield?

All major fabrication happens in our Archerfield workshop. We regularly work with customers from the Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, Ipswich and regional QLD. For very large projects we can discuss on-site consultation.

How is RFC TEC different from other fabricators in Brisbane?

Everything is designed and TIG welded in-house from raw material. No outsourcing, no bolt-on kits, no shortcuts. We only take on work we know we can deliver to the highest standard. Real photos of every finished job are on this site — what you see is what we actually build.
