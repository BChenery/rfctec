About RFCTec

RFCTec, based in Archerfield, Brisbane, specializes in high-quality custom welding and general automotive mechanics for cars. With years of experience, our skilled technicians deliver precision and reliability to keep your vehicle in top condition. We focus on custom welding solutions for structural repairs, modifications, and enhancements, alongside comprehensive mechanical services to ensure your car runs smoothly.

We don’t offer painting services, allowing us to concentrate on what we do best: welding and mechanics. All services are by appointment only, ensuring personalized attention for every job. Our rate is $10/hour (inc GST), providing transparent and competitive pricing for Brisbane’s automotive community.

Our Services

Custom Welding

We provide expert welding services for automotive applications, including structural repairs, exhaust systems, chassis modifications, and custom fabrications. Our precision welding ensures durability and safety for your vehicle.

General Mechanics

From routine maintenance to complex repairs, our general mechanics services cover brakes, suspension, engine diagnostics, and more. We keep your car performing at its best.
