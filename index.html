WE DON'T
BOLT ON.
WE BUILD.

RFCTec is a custom fabrication shop born from a simple belief: the best parts on your car should be the ones that didn't exist before you imagined them.

Every project that leaves this shop starts as raw material and an idea. No catalog parts, no bolt-on kits, no shortcuts. We design, cut, form, fit and weld everything in-house using precision equipment and decades of hands-on experience. From full race car chassis to bespoke street car modifications, we approach every build with the same standard — if we wouldn't put our name on it, it doesn't leave the shop.

WHAT WE DO

Every service is fully custom. We don't do production runs — we do one-at-a-time builds tailored to your vehicle and goals.

01

ROLL CAGE & CHASSIS FABRICATION

FIA/SFI spec cages, custom chassis, subframe design and full structural work.

02

EXHAUST & HEADER SYSTEMS

Custom merge collectors, equal-length headers, turbo manifolds and full exhaust fabrication.

03

SUSPENSION & LINKAGE

Custom control arms, panhard bars, triangulated 4-link setups and full suspension geometry.

04

SHEET METAL & BODYWORK

Fender flares, custom panels, floor replacements, firewall modifications and rust repair.

05

FULL CUSTOM BUILDS

From bare shell to finished car. Complete project management from concept to completion.

HOW IT WORKS

01

CONSULTATION

We discuss your vision, goals, budget and timeline. Bring us an idea or a full CAD file — we'll work with either.

02

DESIGN

We create detailed drawings, mockups and a materials list. You approve everything before a single piece is cut.

03

FABRICATION

This is where the magic happens. Precision cutting, forming, fitting and welding. We send progress photos throughout.

04

DELIVERY

Final fitment, finishing and quality check. Your parts are ready to install — or we handle the full install in-house.
